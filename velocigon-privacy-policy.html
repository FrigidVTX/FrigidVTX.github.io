PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy for VELOCIGON

Last Updated: 9/28/2024

At Neutonium Games, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App, which integrates Unity Ads, a third-party service for advertising. By using the App, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information Collected

We do not collect any personal information directly through the App. However, the Unity Ads SDK may collect certain data types to deliver advertisements and improve the user experience.

a. Location Information

Approximate Location: Unity Ads collects your approximate location based on your device's IP address. This information is used for advertising or marketing, analytics, app functionality, fraud prevention, security, and compliance purposes.

Precise Location: Unity Ads does not collect your precise location.

b. Personal Information

Identifiers: Unity Ads collects personal identifiers such as device IDs to ensure app functionality.

Other Personal Information: Unity Ads does not collect your name, email address, phone number, or other personal information.

c. Financial Information

Purchase History: Unity Ads may collect information about your purchase history in the App. This data is used for advertising or marketing and analytics purposes.

Unity Ads does not collect credit card details or other financial information.

d. App Activity

Page Views and Taps: Unity Ads collects data on page views and taps in the ad experience, but not during gameplay. This data is used for advertising, marketing, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

App Usage Times: Unity Ads collects app usage data to optimize advertisements. This is only enabled if "Acquire Optimization" is turned on in the Unity Monetization dashboard.

e. App Info and Performance

App Diagnostics: Unity Ads collects diagnostic information to help analyze app performance and functionality.

f. Device Identifiers

Unity Ads collects data on your device, including device identifiers, to support advertising, marketing, analytics, app functionality, fraud prevention, security, and compliance.

2. How Collected Data is Used

The data collected by Unity Ads is used to:

  • Deliver personalized advertisements
  • Analyze user behavior to improve ad relevance
  • Monitor app performance and functionality
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Ensure security and compliance with applicable laws

We do not have direct access to the data collected by Unity Ads, but it is shared with Unity and its partners to fulfill these purposes.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure that any data processed by Unity Ads is encrypted in transit and protected against unauthorized access or disclosure. Unity Ads implements security protocols to safeguard user data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Unity Ads for delivering in-app advertisements. Unity Ads may share data with third-party service providers for advertising, analytics, and security purposes. For a complete list of how Unity Ads uses and shares your information, please refer to the Unity Ads Data Safety Page.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that we can take steps to remove such information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review the policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
